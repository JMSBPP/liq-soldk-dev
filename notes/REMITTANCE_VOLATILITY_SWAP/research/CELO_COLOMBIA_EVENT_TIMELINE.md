## July 2025 Spike — Most Likely Explanations

The massive cross-currency spike observed in July 2025 has three strong candidate events occurring within the same month:

1. **2025-07-02: Eclair Testnet launch** — developer burst, bridging tests
2. **2025-07-09: Isthmus Hardfork (15:00 UTC Wednesday)** — highest probability. A mainnet hardfork triggers simultaneous repricing across all Celo stablecoin pairs. Gas parameter changes (Holocene activation) and EIP-7702 account abstraction changes directly affect wallet transaction costs. Exchange and bridge operators rebalance simultaneously. The hardfork lands mid-week, consistent with a spike appearing Thursday or Friday on daily aggregates.
3. **2025-07-17: Governance Call #72** — post-hardfork governance; lower amplitude.

**Recommended cross-check:** Pull Celo block timestamps for July 9, 2025 (block 40172442) and align with the exact UTC timestamp of your on-chain spike. A spike within ±24 hours of 15:00 UTC on July 9 strongly confirms the Isthmus hardfork as the cause.

---

## Thursday Effect — Structural Explanation

Thursdays show elevated on-chain activity because:

1. Celo Governance Calls occur on biweekly Thursdays (confirmed call #42 Feb 15, #46 Apr 25, #49 Jul 18, #53 Oct 24, #72 Jul 17, #88 Apr 2 — all Thursdays)
2. Developer Office Hours run weekly on Thursdays at 10am ET
3. Proof of Ship deadline and submission windows cluster around end-of-month which may fall on Thursdays
4. Post-governance call: wallets that participated in voting unlock after the call, generating a mild transfer wave

This combination makes Thursday the systematically most active day on Celo governance and developer activity cycles.
