# Colombian Calendar Reference: October 2024 - April 2026

**Purpose**: Control variables for econometric regression on daily COP stablecoin flows.

**Data Period**: October 1, 2024 through April 30, 2026 (563 days)

**Legal References**:
- Colombian Labor Code Art. 134: Quincena salary payment on 15th and last day of each month
- Colombian Labor Code Art. 306: Prima de servicios (service bonus) - half by June 30, half by December 20
- Ley Emiliani: Colombian public holidays shifted to following Monday when they fall on weekend

---

## Quincena (Bi-Weekly Salary Payment Days)

**Definition**: Colombian Labor Code Art. 134 mandates salary payment on the 15th and last day of each month. If these dates fall on a weekend or public holiday, payment is typically advanced to the preceding business day.

### 2024 Quincena Dates (Oct-Dec)

October: Oct 15 (Tue), Oct 31 (Thu)
November: Nov 15 (Fri), Nov 29 (Fri) [note: 30th is Sat]
December: Dec 15 (Sun → Dec 13 Fri), Dec 31 (Tue)

### 2025 Quincena Dates

January: Jan 15 (Wed), Jan 31 (Fri)
February: Feb 14 (Fri) [note: 15th is Sat], Feb 28 (Fri)

April: Apr 15 (Tue), Apr 30 (Wed)
May: May 15 (Thu), May 31 (Sat → May 30 Fri)

## Prima de Servicios (Mandatory Service Bonus)

**Definition**: Colombian Labor Code Art. 306 mandates a service bonus equivalent to half a month's salary paid by June 30 and December 20 each year.

### Prima Dates

- **June 30, 2025** (Monday)
- **December 20, 2025** (Friday)
- **June 30, 2026** (Tuesday)

## Data Column Definitions for Regression

When building the time series, use these indicator variables:

| Variable | Description | Values |
|----------|-------------|--------|
| `is_quincena` | Day is salary payment day (15th or last day) | 0, 1 |
| `is_quincena_window` | Day is in ±2 day window around quincena | 0, 1 |
| `is_prima` | Day is prima de servicios payment day | 0, 1 |
| `is_holiday` | Day is Colombian public holiday | 0, 1 |
| `is_weekend` | Day is Saturday or Sunday | 0, 1 |
| `is_thursday` | Day is Thursday (governance/UBI control) | 0, 1 |
| `day_of_week` | ISO weekday (1=Mon, 7=Sun) | 1-7 |
| `day_of_month` | Day of month | 1-31 |
| `month` | Month of year | 1-12 |
| `week_number` | ISO week number | 1-53 |
| `date` | Full date | YYYY-MM-DD |
